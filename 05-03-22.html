How annotations work

Leave feedback right on your asset and enjoy the flexibility to choose how you want to communicate!

  • Use the pencil tool to circle, underline, cross out, draw arrows, and more
  • Customize pencil color and line weight 
  • Toggle on/off annotations or undo 
  • Use on images, PDFs, and videos (pause to comment on an individual frame)
  • Not just drawing! We introduced sticky note annotations as well for precise feedback.

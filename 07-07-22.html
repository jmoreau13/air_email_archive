A new guest invite experience ü§ù 

We heard feedback that our guest and member invite experience was unclear and, at times, contributing to accidental account charges. In June, we made it a priority to more clearly distinguish between the guest and member invite experiences. Here's what changed:

  • New workspace members, who have access to your entire workspace and are always paid seats, can now only be added from your workspace settings (app.air.inc/workspace/members).
  • Guests, who only have access to the board that they have been invited to and can be paid or non-paid seats, can now only be invited at the board level.
  • Any invites that would have an impact on your bill now have a paid seat indicator.
  • In the guest invite modal, we made the free viewer role the default selection.

We hope these updates help you maintain sharing flexibility without any confusion. For a quick refresher on guests, members, and sharing, check out this video.

Custom field notifications
Air supports custom fields on both boards and assets. Now, any time somebody in your workspace changes a custom field value on a board or an asset within a board you follow, you’ll receive a notification. 

Our goal is to make collaborative work as transparent and painless as possible, so you can send less emails and Slack messages. More notifications coming soon!

Expiring share links
Now, whenever you create a share link, you can set it to expire on a date of your choosing.
 
Once a link has expired (at 11:59:59 PM on the day you chose, in the timezone you set it in), it will no longer be accessible to anyone who tries to visit that page, until the expiration date is updated or removed.
 
Note that link expiration is currently only available on a Pro plan or above.

Pro users no longer hit a storage ceiling
Previously, Pro users could only get 2 TB of storage; if you needed more, you’d have to talk to our Sales team about an Enterprise plan. I’ll be the first to admit that was an unnecessary barrier.
 
Now, once your Pro workspace holds more than 2 TB, you'll unlock an additional TB of storage (at $30/month on a monthly Pro plan).

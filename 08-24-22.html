How to turn on Slack notifications

1. In Air, navigate to the board you’d like to connect to Slack 

2. Select Connect to Slack from the ellipsis menu next to the board name

3. Authenticate your Slack workspace and select a channel to receive notifications in

4. Once the integration is configured, all board notifications will be sent to the selected Slack channel

 

Note: At this time, only workspace admins can set up the integration

We’ve been thinking a lot about feedback loops and approval processes lately. Why? Because the best content is the result of true collaboration, which requires honest, direct feedback. We're excited to introduce a few new features that will help teams communicate more effectively on visual projects and see their progress more clearly. Read on find out what’s new this month!

Annotation Options 🖊

Leave clearer, more helpful feedback in less time with our new annotation tool. You can now draw directly on an asset and pin comments for precise feedback. No more gesturing at the screen.

Working with a PDF? Circle, highlight, or underline right on the file. Delivering complex feedback? Doodle right on the asset to express your ideas clearly. Annotations also work on videos, building on our timestamped comment tool. Here’s how it works:

  • Use the pencil tool to highlight, draw, or markup assets
  • Alter markup color and line weight to better express yourself
  • Toggle your annotations on/off, undo actions, or change colors
  • Attach point-based comments to each annotation for additional details

Read more about our new annotation feature here!

Kanban Updates ✨

In kanban view, columns can now be re-ordered by drag and drop or hidden (and re-shown). After hiding or re-ordering, hit Save view to save your changes to the workspace.

 

Searches and sorts can now be applied to a board while in kanban view!

  • Items in hidden columns won’t show up in search results. Searches can be applied by any user (members, guests, public viewers).
  • Items are sorted within columns only (i.e., applying a sort doesn’t affect the order of the columns on the page).

Improved sharing experience 🤝

When you share a board or file with someone, you can now control how long they can see the contents by setting an expiration date on the link when you make it (or even after you’ve already made it). Customers on our Pro and Enterprise plans can add, edit, or remove expiration dates to any board or asset share link.

We also gave our share links a much-needed makeover. The creation process looks cleaner, for one, but we also made them easier to create, manage, and even disable.

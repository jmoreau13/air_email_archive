We're back on Product Hunt and would love your support!

Can we get an upvote?
Some of you may remember Air's Product Hunt launch back in March. We received almost 900 upvotes and were crowned #2 Product of the Day. As it turns out, nothing motivates you quite like getting 2nd place...

Since then, we've been hard at work building out new features to improve the way you collaborate with visual content. That includes:
  • Support for non-visual file types
  • Improved upload and import experience
  • Guest and commenter roles
  • Passcode protected links
  • Table and gallery view
  • and more!
To immortalize this work on the internet forever, today we launched Air 2.0 on Product Hunt. You can check it out here. If you have a second, we'd really appreciate your feedback and support.

You're already familiar with Administrators and Editors, but there are now two new role types exclusively for Air Pro teams: Commenters and Guests.

As the name implies, Commenters can participate in discussions and access the entire workspace with limited edit permissions. Guests have more privileges than Commenters, but can only access boards they've been invited to. 

ðŸ”— Passcode Protected Links

Pro plan members will also have the ability to add a passcode to any asset, board, or file. This allows users to share content outside of their team with additional privacy and security. Passcodes can be toggled on and off by anyone with edit permission.

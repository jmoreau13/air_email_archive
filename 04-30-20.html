Introducing Weekly Drops

We know what you're thinking: "Another newsletter?" No, not another newsletter. Weekly Drops are short, sweet, and full of surprises (only the good kind).

Every Thursday, our team will share a few quick updates about the product and give you a first look at Air's newest drop. Our goal with these is to create a little joy—something fun for you to find in your inbox. No spam, no bullshit, scout's honor. Here are a few examples from earlier this month to get you excited for what's to come:

Support NYC DTC is an interactive, shoppable map of New York. Take a stroll down some of your favorite streets and support businesses founded right here in NYC. 

Zoom Exotic is a collection of over 100+ video backgrounds for your next video chat. Lead a better brainstorm by inviting a group of penguins. Liven up your company all-hands by hosting it at a nightclub. Or a yacht! Or in the middle of a parade! The possibilities are endless.

Can't remember what day today is? You're pretty sure it's Thursday. Unless it's Friday? Calendazed will send you a text every morning with a reminder of the current day of the week.

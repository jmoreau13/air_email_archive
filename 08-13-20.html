On Brand with Britt Cobb

Britt Cobb is an Associate Partner at Pentagram Design. Over the last decade, he's worked on everything from the signage and way-finding on Governors Island to Justin Timberlake's coffee-table book.

When Air underwent a brand refresh at the end of 2019, we called on Britt for assistance. This week, we spoke with him about the visual identity process, as well as his design philosophies at large.

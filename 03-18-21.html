Air Updates | March 2021
From improvements to the import modal to PSD previews, this month's update is all about polish. We smoothed off some of the rough edges and beefed up existing features, allowing you to do even more with your content on Air.

Here's a preview of the changes:
  • Allow guests to have discussions in your workspace by turning on public comments.
     
  • Link your Dropbox, Box, or Google Drive account using our new imports modal. Need to switch between accounts? No problem!
     
  • PSDs now render previews and live alongside the rest of your visual content.
     
  • Add your most frequented boards and assets to your Favorites for easy access.

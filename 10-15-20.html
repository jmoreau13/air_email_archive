Brands Gone Viral 

On September 25th, Nathan Apodaca posted a TikTok of himself skateboarding to work, drinking Ocean Spray, and singing along to Fleetwood Mac's 1977 soft rock classic "Dreams." It wasn't staged, pre-planned, or sponsored — just a man living his best life.

Since then, the video has been viewed over 53 million times, "Dreams" has experienced a resurgence in popularity and, to round out the media exposure, Ocean Spray gifted Nathan with a cranberry-red Nissan pickup truck.

This isn't the first time a brand has ridden the wave of a random viral sensation. This week on the blog, we take a look back at some infamous unsponsored moments in history.
